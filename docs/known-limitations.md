# Known Limitations

- Price and volume use yfinance and can be delayed, unavailable or rate-limited.
- VCP is an objective rolling-window approximation, not visual chart recognition.
- RS is a same-market cross-sectional rating, not the proprietary IBD RS Rating.
- Fundamentals, earnings dates, guidance and news catalysts are not scored.
- Pivot, entry zone and invalidation levels are mechanical estimates and require chart confirmation.
- Tracking uses daily closes from the detection close; it does not reconstruct intraday order of hits.
- The dashboard is informational only and does not place orders.
